Tentacles of ‘0ktapus’ Threat Group Victimize 130 Firms

Severity: 8/10

A massive phishing campaign, dubbed "0ktapus," has compromised 9,931 accounts at over 130 organizations globally, with 114 US-based companies affected. Threat actors targeted employees of companies like Twilio and Cloudflare, sending links to phishing sites that mimicked Okta's authentication page to obtain identity credentials and multi-factor authentication codes.
