Tentacles of ‘0ktapus’ Threat Group Victimize 130 Firms

Severity: 7/10

A massive phishing campaign, dubbed 0ktapus, compromised 9,931 accounts across over 130 companies in 68 countries, primarily targeting US-based firms. The goal was to obtain Okta identity credentials and multi-factor authentication codes via phishing sites mimicking the Okta authentication page. Researchers believe the attack began targeting telecom companies, potentially gaining access to phone numbers used for MFA. The full extent of the attack remains unknown.

Claude Mythos Has Found 271 Zero-Days in Firefox

Severity: 10/10

Mozilla has been collaborating with Anthropic using frontier AI models to find and fix security vulnerabilities in the Firefox browser. This effort has identified 271 latent security bugs, including ones that would have been considered critical in 2025. With the technology favoring defenders, the team is optimistic about keeping up with emerging threats and gaining an upper hand in cybersecurity.
