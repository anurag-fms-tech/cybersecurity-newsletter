Tentacles of ‘0ktapus’ Threat Group Victimize 130 Firms

Severity: 8/10

Over 130 companies, including 114 US-based firms and organizations in 68 countries, were compromised in a massive phishing campaign. Threat actors, dubbed "0ktapus," targeted employees with Okta identity credentials and multi-factor authentication codes by spoofing Okta's authentication system via text messages, resulting in 9,931 compromised accounts. The campaign's full scope remains unknown.
