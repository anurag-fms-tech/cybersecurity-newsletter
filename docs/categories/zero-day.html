Tentacles of ‘0ktapus’ Threat Group Victimize 130 Firms

Severity: 8/10

Over 130 companies worldwide were targeted in a massive phishing campaign that compromised 9,931 accounts. Threat actors exploited identity and access management firm Okta, mimicking its login pages, to obtain multi-factor authentication codes and Okta credentials. The campaign, named "0ktapus", hit 114 US-based firms and others across 68 countries, its full extent still unknown.

Claude Mythos Has Found 271 Zero-Days in Firefox

Severity: 10/10

Firefox collaborated with Anthropic to use AI models to scan the browser for security vulnerabilities. Results found 271 bugs in the latest release, a significant improvement from 22 in previous scans. This technology gives defenders a chance to win against attackers, assuming timely patching and updates can be pushed out to users, and could be a turning point in cybersecurity.
