Ransomware Attacks are on the Rise

Severity: 6/10

Ransomware attacks are back on the rise this summer, led by old groups. Lockbit is the most prolific ransomware gang, followed by Hiveleaks and BlackBasta, two offshoots of the Conti group. With 62 attacks in July, Lockbit maintains its foothold as the most threatening ransomware group. Total ransomware campaigns increased 47% in July, with 198 successful attacks, exceeding pre-May levels.
