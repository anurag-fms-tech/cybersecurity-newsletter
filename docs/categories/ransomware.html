Ransomware Attacks are on the Rise

Severity: 8/10

Lockbit is the most prolific ransomware group this summer, responsible for 62 attacks in July alone. Ransomware attacks have rebounded this summer, with 198 successful campaigns in July, a 47% increase from June and nearing the levels of previous high-water marks.
