Ransomware Attacks are on the Rise

Severity: 8/10

Lockbit was the most prolific ransomware group in July, behind 62 attacks, according to researchers from NCC Group. Lockbit 3.0 maintained its footing as the most threatening group. Other notable groups, Hiveleaks and BlackBasta, saw significant rises with 440% and 50% increases, respectively, and are associated with the dissipated Conti group. Ransomware attacks increased by 47% in July.
