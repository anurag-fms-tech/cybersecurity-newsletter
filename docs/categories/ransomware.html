Ransomware Attacks are on the Rise

Severity: 8 (This rating is due to the increasing threat of ransomware attacks and their potential impact on individuals and organizations.)/10

Lockbit remains the most prolific ransomware group this summer, behind 62 attacks in July. Its resurgence is led by old ransomware-as-a-service (RaaS) groups, with Lockbit 3.0 maintaining its foothold as a top threat. Two offshoots of Conti, Hiveleaks and BlackBasta, experienced rapid rises, with figures suggesting they may be connected to Conti's restructuring.

Cookeville Medical Center Notifies Patients After July 2025 Ransomware Attack

Severity: 8 (Impact severity may be relatively high due to the sensitive nature of medical data and potential health risks, however specific severity can be subjective)/10

Over 337,000 patients of Cookeville Regional Medical Center in Tennessee have been affected by a July 2025 ransomware attack, which exposed their personal and medical data. The hospital began notifying affected individuals nine months later and is offering identity theft protection. The Rhysida ransomware group, linked to Russia, claimed responsibility for the attack and demanded 10 Bitcoin, or approximately $1.15 million.
